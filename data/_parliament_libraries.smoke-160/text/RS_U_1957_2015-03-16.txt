Integrated Education for Disabled Children (a) whether the previous Government had stopped/replaced the scheme of Integrated Education for Disabled Children’ (IEDC), if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; (b) the likely adverse effects on the education of disabled children as a result thereof; and &lt;br/&gt;(c) whether Government is contemplating to restart the said scheme and if so, the details thereof? MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT&lt;br/&gt;(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(a) to (c) The Scheme of “Integrated Education for the Disabled Children (IEDC)” was implemented till 31.3.2009, and thereafter, it has been replaced by the Scheme of “Inclusive Education for the Disabled at Secondary Stage (IEDSS)” which covered IEDC disabled children at both elementary and secondary levels, children with special needs in the elementary stage are now being covered under the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA), and the scheme of IEDSS covers disabled children at the secondary and Higher Secondary stage (class IX-XII). &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;********
