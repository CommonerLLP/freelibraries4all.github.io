Setting up of EMRSs in the country (a) whether Government has decided to set up Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) for tribal students across the country;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise/ UT-wise along with the details of funds released/utilized for the purpose so far especially in Madhya Pradesh;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(c) the time by which these schools are likely to be functional; and&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(d) the steps taken by Government to provide all necessary facilities in EMRSs including proper study atmosphere, class rooms, computer science laboratory, library etc.?&lt;br/&gt; MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TRIBAL AFFAIRS&lt;br/&gt;(SMT. RANEE NARAH)&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(a): Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides a part of grant under the programme of Article 275(1) of the Constitution of India to State governments for setting up of Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) in tribal areas to provide quality education to Scheduled Tribe students from class VI to class XII. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(b): 164 EMRSs have been sanctioned so far to 23 State Governments. Out of this 20 EMRSs have been sanctioned to State of Madhya Pradesh and all of them are functional. Capital cost @ Rs. 12.00 crore per EMRS for construction of school complex, hostels, staff quarters etc., with a provision to go upto Rs. 16.00 crore in hill areas, deserts and islands and recurring cost @ Rs. 42000 per child per annum for the running of each EMRS are provided. Statement of funds released for the EMRSs during last three years is annexed.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(c): Out of the total 164 EMRSs sanctioned by the Ministry so far, 120 EMRSs are already functional and remaining 44 EMRSs are under construction. In the EMRS guidelines, no time line has been prescribed for making the EMRSs functional. However it has been observed that, after release of first installment, the EMRSs generally become functional within a period of 2-3 years.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(d): As per the guidelines issued by Ministry of Tribal Affairs, the facilities like sufficient number of classrooms, Computer/Science laboratory, library/recreation room, housing facilities for the teaching and other supporting staff and hostels with all requisite facilities for the students etc. are to be made available in EMRS. Further, the school building should be accessible to children with disabilities.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;******&lt;br/&gt;
