Reservation for OBCs in Central Universities (a) the details of Assistant Professor positions reserved for OBCs since implementation of Mandal Commission recommendations;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(b) whether it is a fact that all Central Universities particularly University of Hyderabad, have not filled- in the shortfall of teaching positions of OBCs by 27 per cent at the level of Assistant Professor;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(c) if so, the details thereof, University-wise;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(d) whether it is also a fact that University Grants Commission is not monitoring the roster register of each central university; and &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(e) if so, the reasons therefor? MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT&lt;br/&gt;(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(a) to (c) The University Grants Commission (UGC) had issued necessary instructions to all Central Universities for OBC reservation that the 27% OBC reservation is applicable in all Central Educational Institutions in direct recruitment at the Assistant Professor level only w.e.f. 24.1.2007.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; A Statement of Teaching position as on 01.01.2015 (Category-wise) indicating sanctioned/existing/vacant positions in Central Universities under the purview of UGC is at Annexure-I.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(d) to (e) The UGC had issued a circular to all Central Universities with the request to implement the 27% reservation policy of Govt. of India in respect of OBCs for filling up the teaching posts at the level of Lecturer with immediate effect except Minority Institutions under Article 30 (1) of the Constitution of India. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; The UGC is issuing instructions from time to time to all the Universities receiving grant-in-aid form the public fund for (i) implementation of SC/ST/PWD/OBC Reservation Policy of the Govt./UGC and also for (ii) filling up of backlog reserved vacancies of these categories in teaching and non-teaching posts except in Minorities Institutions under Article 30(1) of the Constitution. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; Further, the UGC has since requested all the Central Universities for (i) strict compliance of reservation policy, (ii) display of reservation roster on University’s website and (iii) filling up of remaining identified backlog vacancies for SC/ST/OBC as on 1.11.2008 and Persons with Disabilities as on 15.11.2009 failing which UGC would be constrained to withhold the grant particularly non-plan (non salary) grant.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;*****
