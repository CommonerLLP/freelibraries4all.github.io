Welfare and Rehabilitation Scheme for mentally retarded persons (a) as on date, whether Central Government in consultation with Ministry of Health and Family Welfare and State Governments are going to launch special Welfare and Rehabilitation Scheme for mentally retarded persons, who are spending their precious human life in open to sky and nobody is there to take care of them; and&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(b) whether Central Government has started consultation process with State Governments to address this important social issue?&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; (a) & (b) No, Sir. However, the Government is implementing a Scheme, namely, Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS) under which the Central Government has been providing grant in aid to NGOs for projects relating to rehabilitation to persons with disabilities to enable them to reach and maintain their optimal, physical, sensory, intellectual, psychiatric, or social functional levels.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; Further, the National Trust for Welfare of Persons with Autism, Cerebral Palsy, Mental Retardation and Multiple Disabilities has been implementing a number of schemes for rehabilitation and empowerment of these class of persons with disabilities.&lt;br/&gt; &lt;br/&gt;
